Terms of Service

LAST UPDATED / JULY 2026

Most terms of service pages are dense walls of complicated legal text written to protect companies at your expense. We want Callistra to be transparent, welcoming, and entirely straightforward. Our terms are fundamentally built on mutual respect: we promise to provide simple, private, and helpful tools, and you promise to use them fairly and thoughtfully.

1. Accepting These Terms

By jumping in and using any of the micro-web tools inside Callistra, you are agreeing to these simple structural rules. Because we don't have account creation screens, email verification forms, or long onboarding steps, these guidelines simply outline our shared understanding from the very moment you land on our site.

If these terms don't quite feel right to you, you are always completely free to close the tab and move along to other workflows—no hard feelings whatsoever.

2. Fair Use and Kindness

We work hard to make Callistra completely free, incredibly lightweight, and accessible to creators all over the world. To help us keep this digital playground safe and useful for everyone, we just ask for a few common-sense behaviors from your end:

[ OUR PART / PROVIDE ]

Giving you clean, private code that runs smoothly right inside your own browser window.

[ YOUR PART / RESPECT ]

Using the tools for authentic creative tasks, without trying to scrape, disrupt, or harm the setup.

Please don't use our site or code structure to create automated traffic, try to overload our hosting infrastructure, or engage in any harmful or illegal activities. Let's keep the web an encouraging, productive place for fellow craftsmen.

3. Complete Sovereignty Over Your Work

We want to be entirely clear about this: everything you input into a Callistra tool, modify using our layout scripts, or produce as a final output belongs strictly and exclusively to you. We hold absolutely zero claim, right, or title to your assets, text blocks, or design pieces.

Because our architecture operates 100% locally on your machine, we never store or look at your files. You are always the absolute owner of your craft.

4. Built Thoroughly, Delivered "As-Is"

We take immense pride in our work. We build with care, test our code diligently, and constantly strive to make our tools as helpful and fast as humanly possible. However, because technology changes rapidly and web browsers frequently update their underlying rules, we provide Callistra completely for free and "as-is," without any formal legal warranties.

We cannot formally promise that a tool will look or behave perfectly across every single browser version or complex technical edge case out there. We are not responsible for any minor inconveniences, missed deadlines, or workflow interruptions that might happen along the way. We simply offer these utilities with our best intentions and a warm smile.

5. Updates and Changes

As we design and introduce brand new micro-utilities over time, we may occasionally need to tweak these basic guidelines. We will never quietly switch our model to exploit your data or track your personal work. Any future updates we make will always remain thoroughly faithful to our core local-first manifesto, and we will update the timeline tag right at the top of this page whenever a shift occurs.

THE CORE AGREEMENT

Good software runs on trust and transparency.

We're fully committed to giving you a private, distraction-free environment that helps you build momentum every day. All we ask is that you treat our corner of the web with the same care. Thank you for being a part of Callistra.
